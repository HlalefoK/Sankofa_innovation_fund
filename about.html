Welcome to Sankofa Innovation Fund

25 Years Experience

Sankofa Innovation Fund is passionate about actively driving positive change in Africa by bridging the gap between investors and those with innovative solutions. Sankofa seeks to be a force behind the betterment of the African community by investing in black empowered companies that have high social impact.

Through this vehicle, Sankofa also aims to build sustainable black empowered businesses, facilitate their growth and increase black empowered market leading businesses in key sectors and industries all while increasing investor value.
